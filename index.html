Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Butter for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Heat a pan and grease with butter.
  3. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  4. Cook the other side until golden brown.
  5. Serve with syrup or toppings of choice.

Dum Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup mint and coriander leaves
  • 1/2 cup milk with saffron
  • 2 tbsp ghee

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak Basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate meat with yogurt, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt for 1 hour.
  3. Fry onions in ghee until golden brown and set aside.
  4. Cook marinated meat with tomatoes and spices until tender.
  5. Boil rice until 70% cooked and drain excess water.
  6. Layer cooked meat and rice in a pot, adding fried onions, mint, coriander, and saffron milk.
  7. Cover with a tight lid and cook on low flame (dum) for 20 minutes.
  8. Gently mix and serve hot with raita.

Dosa

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Water as needed
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice and urad dal separately for 6 hours.
  2. Grind both into a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  3. Ferment the batter overnight.
  4. Heat a pan and pour a ladle of batter, spreading it thin.
  5. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
